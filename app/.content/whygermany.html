Advantages to work as a pharmacist in Germany

  • The salary is calculated according to the tariff for pharmacy workers and starts from 3,500 euros. If you have a work record, there are allowances
  • Standardized work schedule from 20 to 40 hours per week
  • Sundays and public holidays are days off, except for day or night shifts (shared with other pharmacies in the city)
  • Paid vacation (5 weeks on average)
  • Paid sick leave
  • Regular training programs for professional development
  • In many pharmacies, employees with children receive kindergarten fees, also pay for transportation costs, etc
  • Often, a pharmacy has a robotic vending machine in which medicines are stored and which then delivers them to the sales floor at the request of an employee. This reduces the time to find the necessary drug and allows you to devote more time to the patient
